Privacy Policy for Ctrl+RightClick Element Copier

Last Updated: 2025 December 17

1. Information We Collect
Our extension does not collect any personal information, browsing history, or usage analytics. The only data accessed is the HTML (outerHTML) of a specific page element that you explicitly interact with via the Ctrl+RightClick gesture. This access occurs only at the moment of your action and is limited strictly to the element you’ve selected.

2. How We Use Information
The captured HTML is copied directly to your system clipboard for your immediate use (e.g., debugging, web development). We do not process, store, analyze, or transmit this data. The extension’s functionality ends the moment the HTML is written to your clipboard.

3. Why We Collect Information
We access HTML solely to perform the core function you’ve requested: copying an element’s HTML structure. Without accessing this specific data, the extension cannot operate as designed.

4. Data Storage
Our extension does not store any data. The HTML copied from your selected element is transferred only to your clipboard and is not retained, logged, or persistently stored by the extension. No internal databases or servers retain this information.

5. Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties. The HTML copied to your clipboard remains entirely under your control and is never transmitted, uploaded, or exposed to external servers or services.

6. Your Control
All data access requires your explicit action. The extension remains idle until you perform the Ctrl+RightClick gesture. You can disable or remove the extension anytime via Chrome’s Extensions page to revoke all access immediately.

7. Scripts & Permissions
The extension uses a lightweight content script that runs only in your active tab to detect your gesture and access the clicked element. This script does not run in the background and cannot access pages you are not actively using.

隐私政策

1. 我们收集的信息
我们的扩展程序不会收集任何个人信息、浏览历史记录或使用分析数据。唯一访问的数据是您通过 Ctrl+右键点击手势明确交互的特定页面元素的HTML（outerHTML）。此访问仅在您执行操作时发生，并严格限于您选择的元素。

2. 我们如何使用信息
捕获的HTML会直接复制到您的系统剪贴板供您立即使用（例如，调试、网页开发）。我们不会处理、存储、分析或传输此数据。扩展程序的功能在HTML写入您的剪贴板后立即结束。

3. 我们收集信息的原因
我们访问HTML仅是为了执行您请求的核心功能：复制元素的HTML结构。如果不访问此特定数据，扩展程序无法按设计运行。

4. 数据存储
我们的扩展程序不存储任何数据。从您选择的元素中复制的HTML仅传输到您的剪贴板，不会被扩展程序保留、记录或持久存储。没有内部数据库或服务器保留此信息。

5. 数据共享
我们不会与第三方共享任何数据。复制到您剪贴板的HTML完全由您控制，绝不会传输、上传或暴露给外部服务器或服务。

6. 您的控制
所有数据访问都需要您明确操作。在您执行Ctrl+右键点击手势之前，扩展程序保持闲置状态。您可以随时通过Chrome的扩展程序页面禁用或移除此扩展程序，立即撤销所有访问权限。

7. 脚本与权限
扩展程序使用轻量级内容脚本，仅在您活动的标签页中运行，以检测您的手势并访问被点击的元素。此脚本不会在后台运行，也无法访问您未主动使用的页面。
